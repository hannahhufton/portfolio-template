The Education department of my company was applying for a brand new $1.5M government grant. They had created a 25 page project charter for a year-long oversees filmmaking mentorship program and asked me to create the budget which needed to be submitted for the grant application. I took the 25 page description of the program and created an actionable budget and a cost management plan for the year-long program. We wound up winning the grant and continued to receive the grant for the next 4 years. After the grant was received, I helped the newly hired program manager to learn and maintain the budget.
